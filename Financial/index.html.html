Financail Cleaning Made Easy With Prioity Janitorial Service

Bee-Clean is Canada’s largest cleaning service provider to Banks and other financial institutions currently cleaning over 2,210 individual bank locations on a nightly basis. We understand that a clean, well maintained branch or office enhances a customer’s confidence and adds to our clients’ guest management expectations. Our unparalleled expertise in financial sector cleaning means we have an intimate understanding of the relationship between cleaning, scheduling, security and environmental mandates that ensure we provide a national standard of consistent cleanliness. Bee-Clean crews are enhanced security cleared and trained to provide the specialized service required by the financial sector. They are trained to adhere to each facility’s privacy and security protocols and are highly compliant with the confidential and secure nature of the financial environment. Our teams can work during or after business hours, according to the needs of each branch and office.
